WHITE DIAMOND
VARIATIONS

Since 1906, diamonds have lit up the Maison's pieces with their sparkle. Today, Van Cleef & Arpels reveals them in all their facets with the High Jewelry second chapter Legend of diamonds - White diamond variations. A homage to the Maison's creative history and the timeless beauty of this precious stone.

Roaring twenties transformable long necklace. White gold, rose gold, one DIF Type 2A rose-cut pear diamond of 10.32 carats, one DIF rose-cut oval diamond of 4.46 carats, diamonds.

New haricot clip, Spontini ring and Boogie-woogie bracelet

Symbols of purity and eternity, diamonds emphasize the precious character of the collection. Chosen in a variety of cuts – round, baguette-cut, marquise-cut, rose-cut and pear-shaped – they have been selected to meet the especially high standards of the Maison's gemmological experts.

Inspired by the Maison's rich heritage, the White diamond variations collection's pieces retrace its creative history, reinterpreting its distinctive style to reflect different eras and artistic trends.
